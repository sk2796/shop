A Legacy of Pure Devotion

Located at the West Gate of the sacred Baba Baidyanath Temple, Shri Chhote Lal Ganga Ram Sah Peda Bhandar has been a cornerstone of Deoghar's spiritual fabric for eight generations.

What began as a small offering to Lord Shiva has grown into a legacy of trust and artisanal excellence. Each peda is crafted using time-honored techniques passed down through our family, ensuring that every bite carries the same purity and devotion that defined our shop since its inception.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shelf life of the pedas? expand_more

Our traditional pedas stay fresh for up to 15 days at room temperature. We recommend keeping them in a cool, dry place or refrigerating them for extended freshness up to 25 days.

Do you offer delivery to my city? expand_more

Yes! We provide white-glove delivery across all major cities in India via premium courier partners. Most orders reach within 2-4 business days.

Can a batch be offered at the Temple as Prasad? expand_more

Absolutely. As we are located right at the West Gate, we can arrange for specific 'Bhog' pedas to be offered to Baba Baidyanath through our designated priests upon request.

Are you open during all temple hours? expand_more

Yes, our shop operates from 4:00 AM to 10:30 PM daily, ensuring pilgrims can obtain fresh Prasad from the first 'Arghya' to the final 'Shringar Puja'.
